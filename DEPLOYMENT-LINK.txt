========================================
  HOW TO GET YOUR DEPLOYMENT URL
========================================

Method 1: Vercel Dashboard
---------------------------
1. Go to https://vercel.com/dashboard
2. Click on your project
3. Copy the deployment URL from the top
4. It will look like: https://project-name.vercel.app

Method 2: From Deployment Email
--------------------------------
Check your email for Vercel deployment notification
The URL will be in the email

Method 3: From Browser
-----------------------
If you opened the deployment, check your browser address bar
